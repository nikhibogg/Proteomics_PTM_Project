##S Pneumoniae Active sites.csv. 
This corresponds to the data obtained from our positive phosphorylation datastet.
As the results where only in the form of a pdf, all of the Genbank ID's were manually copied into a text string, then ran through the NCBI Entrez efetch API
To obtain a series of corresponding Proteins and Accesion numbers.
This was then also filtered manually to arrive at this result.
